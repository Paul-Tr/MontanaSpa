About

For those looking for enchanting " Walk in. Float out. " experiences, we have created a unique place in the mountains, where relaxation and wellbeing await.

You'll find all you need to clear your mind and revitalize your body. Indulge in the Montana SPA experience, complete with heated indoor pool (9x6 m, 32 degrees C, 1.35 m depth), jacuzzi with a 20 person capacity (36 degrees C), infrared sauna, 2 steam baths (one with aromatherapy), salt grotto, Finnish sauna and relaxation zone.

Our SPA is located in the beautiful and quiet Colibița mountain village, in Transylvania. You'll enjoy the untamed charm of the surroundings, with mountains, forests and lake while breathing the famous crisp, pure air Colibița is officially recognized for. We also guarantee a complete lack of vampires in the area and suggest leaving the garlic at home.
